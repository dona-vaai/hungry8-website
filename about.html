About Hungry8

Born from too many "I don't know, what do you want?" conversations.

The Problem

Decision fatigue is real

We've all been there. It's 6 PM, you're hungry, and the most exhausting conversation begins: "Where do you want to eat?"

You scroll through delivery apps. Too many choices. You suggest something. Shot down. They suggest something. Not in the mood. Twenty minutes later, you're still hangry and no closer to food.

The average person makes 35,000 decisions per day. Why waste any of them on something that should be simple?

The Solution

Let fate decide

What if you could just... shake it off? Like a Magic 8-Ball, but for food.

That's Hungry8. Shake your phone, get a restaurant. One that matches your taste, is actually open right now, and is close enough to get to. No endless scrolling. No decision paralysis.

One shake. One pick. Done.

The Mission

Give you back your time

We're building Hungry8 to eliminate the time you waste deciding where to eat. Whether you're alone, with friends, or trying to end a 30-minute debate with your partner — one shake, and the decision is made.

Life's too short to spend it scrolling through restaurant apps. There are better things to do with those 20 minutes. Like actually eating.
