Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

At Hungry8, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Location data: Used only to find nearby restaurants. We don't store your location history.
  • Taste preferences: Your food preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To find restaurants near you
  • To personalize recommendations based on your taste profile
  • To improve our app and service

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your personal information
  • We don't share your data with advertisers
  • We don't track your location when the app is closed

Data Storage

Your taste preferences are stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Places API to provide restaurant information. Their privacy policy applies to data they collect.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@hungry8.com
